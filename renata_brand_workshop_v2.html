The symbol is the Aperture

A renewal analyst reads the whole field and narrows it to one thing worth your attention. So the symbol is a lens stopping down: a faceted iris that doubles as the counter of the R. It is a designed object, not a bullet.

An iris: four facets that close to one focus. When the facets sit square, the field is in focus and Renata has found the one account that matters.

It reads as a lens, an eye, and a cut gem at once: the instrument, the attention, and the value. It nests exactly inside the bowl of the R, so the letter and the symbol are one form. And it gives us the whole behaviour system: the iris seats square when resolved, and rotates open while it hunts.

Alignment is the system

The R never moves. Only the iris changes: its rotation, its drift from home, and its color. Aligned is the resting truth the mark always returns to. Every state is a deviation from it, resolving back.

The one rule
The R-structure is immovable. Only the iris speaks, through three variables: rotation (square vs open), drift (home vs off), and color (violet present, teal found, amber timing, red risk). One variable leads each state, motion is slow and eased, and every state has a static end-frame so the mark tells the truth with animation off.
