The one rule
In the app, the whole mark carries the state through colour: violet while she works, teal the instant an account is found, amber on a renewal clock, red when something is at risk. Motion is the second read, slow for the calm states and sharp for the urgent one. On letterhead and decks the mark always rests in its brand violet or ink. Every state holds a still end-frame for reduced motion.
