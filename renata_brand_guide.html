Voice & tone

Specific, generous, calm.

Speak in specifics. Months, dollars, carriers — never an adjective where a number will do.
Generous first, sells last. The insight is the headline. The product is the P.S.
Calm authority. No hype, no urgency theater, no emoji clutter. She knows things.
Show the work. Always say why an account is in play. Legibility is how she earns trust.

Sounds like Renata

  • "Renews in July, paying 22% over comparable Austin employers."
  • "I drafted the intro. Send as you, or edit first."
  • "You closed three last quarter — switch to flat and save $1,350."

Never Renata

  • "🔥 HOT LEAD you can't afford to miss!!"
  • "Leverage our AI-powered synergistic platform."
  • Data in serif, or her voice in mono. Keep the lanes.
