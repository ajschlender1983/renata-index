The mark, and how to use it.

A solid R, drawn as a letter. In the app it doubles as Renata's status light: the whole mark takes the state colour, so what she is doing reads at a glance, down to a 16-pixel tab. This page is the source of truth; the files live in /brand.

Construction & clear space

Keep clear space equal to half the mark's height on every side. Never render the mark below 16px, and never redraw it: pull from /brand.

Clear space

Half the mark height, all sides. Nothing intrudes.

Minimum size

It holds from a hero down to a favicon. 16px is the floor.

The six states

The whole mark carries the state through colour, legible down to a 16px tab. The three violet states are one calm family, told apart by motion. See the live demo on the mark page.

At app and favicon size
presentviolet
workingviolet · throb
foundteal
timingamber
alertred
Accessibility. Never signal a state by colour alone. Pair it with the motion and an adjacent text label, e.g. "1 account found". Reduced-motion users get the still end-frame.

05

Do & don't

Do

  • Pull the mark from /brand; never redraw it.
  • Let the whole mark carry the live state colour in the app.
  • Keep the lockup in ink or violet on every surface.
  • Pair every state with motion and a text label.

Don't

  • Recolour the aperture on its own, or add a glow.
  • Stretch, rotate, or re-proportion the R.
  • Tint the logo lockup with a state colour.
  • Render below 16px or crowd the clear space.
