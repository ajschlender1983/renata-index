Brand Workshop IV · Bolder

The same idea, struck harder.

Five bolder ways to draw the circular R. The aperture stays the soul of the mark, but now it lives in solid mass, negative space, and die-struck weight. Each one is built to look minted rather than drawn.

The boldest · Eclipse
A solid disc with the aperture cut from the mass. The iris is a window you see intelligence through.

Five bolder takes

From line to mass. Solid bowls, cut apertures, a die-struck seal, and a dominant iris. Each keeps the R, the aperture, and the field flowing in, and each pushes the weight much harder.
